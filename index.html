Make a Grilled Cheese Sandwich!

Learn how to make a simple Grilled Cheese Sandwich!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • cheese
  • sandwich maker

Instructions

  1. Lay out two slices of bread.
  2. Cut your cheese into strips
  3. Apply cheese on both slices.
  4. Put the two slices of bread together
  5. Put inside a sandwich maker
